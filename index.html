Wisdom of the Navajo / Diné Nation

Learn and Share the Wisdom!

The transmission of tribal teachings is often done orally and is deeply rooted in the community's traditions and cultural context. Additionally, respect for the sensitivity of tribal knowledge is crucial. For a comprehensive and accurate understanding of Navajo / Diné teachings, I had to reach out to the Navajo Nation directly, consult with their elders, cultural experts, or official tribal sources. They are the most authoritative and authentic sources of information regarding the specific teachings, ceremonies, and cultural practices of the Navajo / Diné people.
